About the Event ✨

The School Annual Function is one of the most awaited events of the year. It provides a platform for students to showcase their talents in dance, music, drama, and academics.

Our Theme 🌟

This year’s theme is “Celebrating Creativity & Excellence”. The event highlights innovation, teamwork, leadership, and artistic skills of our students.

Message from the Principal 🎓

“Education is not only about academics but also about overall personality development. Our Annual Function reflects the dedication and creativity of our students and teachers.”
